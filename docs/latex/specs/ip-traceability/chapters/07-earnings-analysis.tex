\section{Concept: Constitutional AI Framework}
\label{sec:ip006-constitutional}

\textit{``Prometheus Unbound Constitutional Monitoring: The Prometheus Unbound framework provides comprehensive oversight through its tensor-based validation engine, calibrated explicitly for earnings call analysis. Operating within strict regulatory boundaries, the system implements multi-tiered monitoring through interconnected validation nodes.''}
\end{ipsourcebox}

\begin{tracerecord}
\textbf{IP Source ID} & IP-006 \\
\textbf{IP Location} & Processing Architecture Section \\
\textbf{Concept} & Constitutional AI for compliance monitoring \\
\midrule
\textbf{Implementation Path} & \filepath{src/intelligence/ai-core-pal/governance/} \\
\textbf{Adaptation Type} & Conceptual (governance framework) \\
\end{tracerecord}

\begin{adaptbox}
The IP paper describes Prometheus Unbound as a constitutional AI monitoring framework. This concept is adapted in SAP-OSS through the governance layer, which implements multi-tiered validation similar to the constitutional boundaries described. The \funcname{GovernedAgent} and \funcname{SafetyGate} classes provide the ``validation nodes'' that ensure compliance before actions are executed.
\end{adaptbox}

\end{ipsourcebox}

\begin{tracerecord}
\textbf{IP Source ID} & IP-006 \\
\textbf{IP Location} & Technical Architecture Section \\
\textbf{Concept} & Integration of multiple analysis modalities \\
\midrule
\textbf{Implementation Path} & \filepath{src/intelligence/} \\
\textbf{Adaptation Type} & Structural (analytics pipeline) \\
\end{tracerecord}

\begin{adaptbox}
The IP paper describes multi-modal analysis combining voice, text, and metrics. The SAP-OSS analytics pipeline adapts this concept through modular services: OCR for document processing, vLLM for text analysis, and the analytics module for numerical metrics. While not processing voice, the multi-modal principle of integrating diverse data types is preserved.
\end{adaptbox}

\section{Concept: Confidence Metrics}
\label{sec:ip006-confidence}

\begin{ipsourcebox}{IP-006, Insight Generation}
\textit{``The system provides transparent reasoning and confidence metrics for all insights, enabling informed decision-making while maintaining clear audit trails of analytical processes.''}
\end{ipsourcebox}

\begin{tracerecord}
\textbf{IP Source ID} & IP-006 \\
\textbf{IP Location} & Insight Generation Section \\
\textbf{Concept} & Quantified reliability for outputs \\
\midrule
\textbf{Implementation Path} & \filepath{src/intelligence/analytics/anomaly\_detector.py} \\
\textbf{Adaptation Type} & Structural (score fields) \\
\end{tracerecord}

\begin{adaptbox}
The IP paper emphasizes confidence metrics for all insights. The SAP-OSS implementation provides this through the \texttt{score} field in \funcname{AnomalyResult} (Z-score as confidence measure) and the \texttt{anomaly\_score} in \funcname{VarianceAnalysisResult}. Higher scores indicate higher confidence in the anomaly classification.
\end{adaptbox}
