\chapter{Risk Scoring Model}
\label{ch:risk}

\section{Purpose of risk scoring}

The risk scoring model provides a quantitative, repeatable method to:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Classify use cases into risk tiers (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
  \item Determine applicable control profiles
  \item Calculate residual risk after controls are applied
  \item Gate production deployment based on numeric thresholds
\end{enumerate}

\section{Inherent Risk Score (IRS)}

The \textbf{Inherent Risk Score} for use case $u$ is the weighted sum of
normalized risk factors:

\begin{equation}
\boxed{
IRS_u = 100 \times \left(
  0.20 \cdot DS_u + 
  0.20 \cdot AA_u + 
  0.15 \cdot AU_u + 
  0.15 \cdot EC_u + 
  0.10 \cdot RV_u + 
  0.10 \cdot UI_u + 
  0.05 \cdot MU_u + 
  0.05 \cdot TD_u
\right)
}
\label{eq:irs}
\end{equation}

\subsection{Worked example: UC-10 Agent/tool orchestration}

\begin{align*}
DS &= 0.70 \quad \text{(confidential data)} \\
AA &= 0.80 \quad \text{(external writes via MCP)} \\
AU &= 0.70 \quad \text{(human approves critical only)} \\
EC &= 1.00 \quad \text{(MCP/browser connectivity)} \\
RV &= 0.60 \quad \text{(partially reversible)} \\
UI &= 0.80 \quad \text{(high user impact)} \\
MU &= 1.00 \quad \text{(multi-agent)} \\
TD &= 0.50 \quad \text{(controlled vendor)}
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
IRS &= 100 \times (0.20 \times 0.70 + 0.20 \times 0.80 + 0.15 \times 0.70 + 0.15 \times 1.00 \\
    &\quad + 0.10 \times 0.60 + 0.10 \times 0.80 + 0.05 \times 1.00 + 0.05 \times 0.50) \\
    &= 100 \times (0.14 + 0.16 + 0.105 + 0.15 + 0.06 + 0.08 + 0.05 + 0.025) \\
    &= 100 \times 0.77 = \mathbf{77}
\end{align*}

Result: \textbf{Critical tier} ($IRS \geq 75$).

\section{Applicability-Weighted Control Coverage (AWCC)}

Let $a_{u,j} \in \{0,1\}$ indicate whether control $j$ applies to use case $u$.
Let $p_{u,j}$ be the control outcome score:

\[
p_{u,j} = \begin{cases}
1.0 & \text{control passes} \\
0.5 & \text{partial or compensating pass} \\
0.0 & \text{control fails}
\end{cases}
\]

The \textbf{Applicability-Weighted Control Coverage} is:

\begin{equation}
\boxed{
AWCC_u = \frac{\sum_{j=1}^{23} a_{u,j} \cdot p_{u,j}}{\sum_{j=1}^{23} a_{u,j}}
}
\label{eq:awcc}
\end{equation}

\section{Penalty terms}

Penalty terms add fixed risk points for specific control failures:

\begin{equation}
\boxed{
Penalty_u = 15 \cdot N^{audit}_u + 15 \cdot N^{checkpoint}_u + 10 \cdot N^{external}_u + 20 \cdot N^{privacy}_u
}
\label{eq:penalty}
\end{equation}

where:
\begin{itemize}
  \item $N^{audit}_u = 1$ if full audit trace is missing (\controlid{MON-01} fails), else $0$
  \item $N^{checkpoint}_u = 1$ if a required human checkpoint is missing
        (\controlid{HUM-01} fails), else $0$
  \item $N^{external}_u = 1$ if untrusted external tools/services are used
        (\controlid{SEC-02} fails), else $0$
  \item $N^{privacy}_u = 1$ if personal-data handling lacks lawful basis
        (\controlid{DATA-02} fails), else $0$
\end{itemize}

\section{Residual Risk Score (RRS)}

The \textbf{Residual Risk Score} combines inherent risk, control coverage, and
penalties:

\begin{equation}
\boxed{
RRS_u = \min\left(100, \; IRS_u \times (1 - 0.55 \cdot AWCC_u) + Penalty_u\right)
}
\label{eq:rrs}
\end{equation}

The $0.55$ coefficient means that perfect control coverage ($AWCC = 1.0$)
reduces inherent risk by 55\%.

\section{Deployment Readiness Score (DRS)}

The \textbf{Deployment Readiness Score} is a composite metric combining
evaluation results, residual risk, control coverage, and training:

\begin{equation}
\boxed{
DRS_u = 0.4 \cdot EVAL_u + 0.3 \cdot (100 - RRS_u) + 0.2 \cdot (AWCC_u \times 100) + 0.1 \cdot TC_u
}
\label{eq:drs}
\end{equation}

where:
\begin{itemize}
  \item $EVAL_u$ is the evaluation score (see Chapter~\ref{ch:metrics})
  \item $RRS_u$ is the residual risk score
  \item $AWCC_u$ is the control coverage
  \item $TC_u$ is the training coverage percentage
\end{itemize}

\section{Production gate summary}

A use case $u$ is eligible for production deployment if and only if:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item $RRS_u \leq 45$ (residual risk within tolerance)
  \item $DRS_u \geq 70$ (deployment readiness met)
  \item All critical controls pass (see Chapter~\ref{ch:controls})
  \item All metric $LCB_{95}$ values meet thresholds (see Chapter~\ref{ch:metrics})
\end{enumerate}

All four conditions must be satisfied; failing any one blocks production
deployment.
