\section{Modifications History}

This section previously listed line-by-line modification timelines for services
that included optional native streaming and rule-engine integrations. Those
components are no longer maintained in-tree; the authoritative record of
current changes is \texttt{git log} and per-service \texttt{README} files under
\texttt{src/}.

SAP OSS services documented in \texttt{SAP-OSS-SBOM-Comprehensive.tex} are
tracked via CycloneDX and SPDX manifests under
\texttt{scripts/sbom-lineage/boms/}. Use \texttt{make sbom-sarif} for SARIF
export of SBOM policy findings.
