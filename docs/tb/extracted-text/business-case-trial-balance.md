1. Executive Summary


Trial Balance review is conducted during M+1 to M+7 across all Legal
entities, Segment & Group, to understand the variance & balance
movements between periods. Deep dives to assess the events,
transactions causing the variances and reasons behind them. Analysed
with detailed commentaries for these movements are prepared &
submitted to Group for further review & used for Country CFO
packs/Legal entity reporting.


Downloading Trail balance from PSGL/S4 & preparing the detailed
analysis across all 234 LEs are highly time consuming, current view is
at Legal entity level and need to be in Finance caption level &
segment level. Considerable time spent during month end close days and
limiting the time for deep dives & detailed analysis


An AI model produces business insights and written commentaries
(Finance reporting caption Level & Segment Level with Legal entity
view) based on the analysis performed on general ledger transaction
data that detects anomalies, trends, unusual movements, new accounts,
spikes/drops vs prior periods, and material balances. GCFO teams get a
narrative summary instead of just numbers, accelerating review and
improving decision-making. Template should have multiple user access
for further inputs/review.


At least 20 to 25 FTEs spent on an average 4 hrs a day during M-2 to
M+5, which is equivalent to ~5 FTE saves, with strengthening controls
by eliminating manual process and reducing the overall book close
timelines enabling Fast close.


AI Architect & AI Engineer from SCB AI Studios required to spent <100
days to build this AI model, along with GPM, Process lead, SME & PPE
lead for at least 10 to 15 days equivalent efforts for Business
Requirements, designing process models, validating the solutions
created and perform testing to confirm results.


Recommended to proceed with Studio AI solutions which is inhouse and
cost & timelines are minimal.

2. Problem/Opportunity statement


## Current state:
• Trial Balance variance analysis with commentaries for 234 LEs, Segments &
Group is performed during M+1 to M+7 days & shared with Group team for
review. (Refer ARIS process map for detailed steps)
• Currently it is only with reporting dimension of Legal Entity and team
manually prepares Finance caption level reporting which takes considerable
time on data extraction & preparation of reports rather than Analysis &
insightful commentaries
• Over all delays in reporting process to Group as well as Countries
• Current state is not ready for moving into daily controls


## Business impact:
• 20 to 25 FTEs manual efforts reduction in Trial Balance preparation &
review during M+1 to M+7
• Hurdle to achieve overall reporting timelines
• Manual process leading to EUCs & related maintenance; manual handoffs
leading to continuous monitoring of controls, enhanced automated controls
from UK ACG perspective
• Foundation for future daily-control operating model, enabling real-time
financial insight.


## Drivers for Change:
• In house AI solutions
• AI teams availability
• Solution feasibility
• Deployment timelines &
• No/minimal additional cost for AI solutions & maintenance (esp. no cost
for LLM, Tokens, AI team)


3. Project Description


## Proposed Solution:
An AI model produces business insights and written commentaries in the
trial balance review views based on the analysis performed on general
ledger transaction data that detects anomalies, trends, unusual
movements, new accounts, spikes/drops vs prior periods, and material
balances. GCFO teams get a narrative summary instead of just numbers,
accelerating review and improving decision-making.




Project Objectives
• Optimisation of process to improve the process maturity levels
• E2E process automation including commentary writing
• Building block to create SCB Finance LLM


Scope
• In scope – Trial balance review at caption level, variance analysis &
commentary writing
• Out of scope – Country IFRS reporting, BSS Risk assessment


Key deliverables
• AI automation on Trial balance process
