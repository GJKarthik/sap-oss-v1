1. Executive Summary


BSS Risk Assessment is conducted during M+12 to M+15 across all Legal
entities, Segment & Group. From M+12 to M+ 15, follow up is done and
the tracker updating happens.
➢ Manual consolidation of the BSS Risk Assessment by different categories
– substantiated at risk, unsubstantiated unowned, unsubstantiated owned.
➢ Manual follow up and consolidation of manual submissions post FCA
cutoff. Triggers escalation of the unsubstantiated via emails.
➢ Current reporting does not have a caption view/ segment view/ legal
entity view and is needed for the country reporting, risk forums and audit
committee forums.
➢ Additional manual efforts spent on above activities.


AI agent assists users by highlighting high-risk GL accounts,
suggesting risk ratings and proposing justification based on analysis
of reconciliation data, adjustment activity inside the FC App and
transaction data in general ledger. It detects anomalies in balances,
ageing, recurring adjustments, historical trends or policy thresholds.
enabling faster and more informed financial control decisions.


The BSS risk assessment is a critical Financial reporting (risk)
process used for CFO attestation as well as Risk /Audit committee
reporting, and thereby it is critical for automation.
At least 10 to 12 FTEs efforts is spent during M+12 to M+15, which is
equivalent to 1 FTE saves.


AI Architect & AI Engineer from SCB AI Studios required to spent <100
days to build this AI model, along with GPM, Process lead, SME & PPE
lead for at least 10 to 15 days equivalent efforts for Business
Requirements, designing process models, validating the solutions
created and perform testing to confirm results.


Recommended to proceed with Studio AI solutions which is inhouse and
cost & timelines are minimal.

2. Problem/Opportunity statement
## Current state:
• BSS Risk Assessment analysis with commentaries for 234 Les & Group is
performed during M+12 to M+15 days & shared with CFOs, Risk Forums and
Audit committee (Refer VISIO process map for detailed steps)
• Highly manual process, with considerable time spent on consolidation on
risk assessed file at a group level at legal entity to create a
consolidated group view.
• Manually updating unsubstantiated report daily (M+13 to M+15) to reflect
the manual submissions received post FCA cutoffs.
• Manual time spent on preparing the risk reporting packs.


## Business impact:
• Improved and efficient risk reporting across CFO, Risk forum and audit
committee.
• 1 FTE manual efforts reduction in BSS Risk Assessment process
• Enhanced UKACG reporting and risk monitoring.


## Drivers for Change:
• In house AI solutions
• AI teams availability
• Solution feasibility
• Deployment timelines &
• No additional cost for AI solutions (esp. no cost for LLM, Tokens, AI
team)


3. Project Description
## Proposed Solution:
AI agent assists users by highlighting high-risk GL accounts,
suggesting risk ratings and proposing justification based on analysis
of reconciliation data, adjustment activity inside the FC App and
transaction data in general ledger. It detects anomalies in balances,
ageing, recurring adjustments, historical trends or policy thresholds.
enabling faster and more informed financial control decisions.


Project Objectives
• Optimisation of process to improve the process maturity levels
• E2E process automation including Risk Assessment & identification of
material items
• Building block to create SCB Finance LLM


Scope
• In scope – BSS Risk Assessment
• Out of scope – FORTM, BSS Reconciliations


Key deliverables
• AI automation on BSS Risk Assessment
